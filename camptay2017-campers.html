CAMP TAY 2017

Thai American Youth Camp

August 25th - 26th, 2017

Hey campers! This year we are going on our 6th year holding our annual Camp เท่ (Thai American Youth). The camp is about helping our campers develop leadership skills, promote teamwork, develop strengths in their character, learn about their Thai heritage and their identity as Thai-Americans. Mostly importantly, we want our campers to have fun!

Leadership

Teamwork

Character

Thai American Pride

Details:

When

9am Saturday, August 25th - 5pm Sunday, August 26th

Where

Wat Buddhanusorn

36054 Niles Blvd

Fremont, CA 94536

Google Map

Cost and registration deadlines:

• $75 if registered by Sunday, August 6, 2017

• $100 if registered after that

Camp cost: This will cover the cost of food, supplies, and a camp T-shirt. Additional monetary or food donations are kindly appreciated. Unlike last year, there will be no optional packages. This is for two days and one overnight. Actual value of the camp is $150 per camper, but we are able to offer a reduced price thanks to our sponsors and volunteers.

Age Restriction: Youngest accepted is 10 years old. Oldest accepted are graduating 12th graders for Spring 2017. Please talk to us directly for possible age exceptions. They may be made on a case by case basis.

What to bring:

Sleeping bag

Sleeping pillow (if have one)

Warm clothes for night time

Flashlight

Change of clothes for 2 days

Hygiene items (e.g. toothbrush and toothpaste, soap, towels etc.)

Medication (if needed)

Overnight details: Sleeping in tents in the backlot. Parents are allowed to sleep over as well, but will be required to fill out the temple's Child Protection Policy. Please contact us for more details.
