Thai American Youth Camp

Promoting pride as Thai-American

Camp (Thai American Youth) or Camp เท่ is not for profit and is run by volunteers consisting of Thai American young professionals and college students. The goals of the camp are to foster character development, leadership, teamwork, and Thai American pride for both volunteers and campers.
