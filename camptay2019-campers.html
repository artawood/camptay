CAMP TAY 2019

Thai American Youth Camp

August 24th - 25th, 2019

Hey campers! This year we are going on our 8th year holding our annual Camp เท่ (Thai American Youth). The camp is about helping our campers develop leadership skills, promote teamwork, develop strengths in their character, learn about their Thai heritage and their identity as Thai-Americans. Mostly importantly, we want our campers to have fun!

Leadership

Teamwork

Details:

When

Saturday, August 24th - Sunday, August 25th

Camp Schedule

Saturday, August 24

• 8:30AM - Camp TAY begins at Wat Buddhanusorn

• 9:30AM - Bus leaves from Wat Buddhannusorn for Rob Hill Camp ground in San Francisco

Sunday, August 25

• 12:00PM - Bus arrives from Rob Hill Camp ground arrives at Wat Buddhanusorn

• 5:00PM - End of Camp TAY

Locations

• Begin and end at Wat Buddhanusorn

• Majority spent at Rob Hill Camp ground at the Presidio (near the Golden Gate Bridge) in San Francisco
- Campsites HUYA & YUNAHIA

• Hike to Baker Beach

Cost and registration deadlines:

• $75 if registered by Sunday, July 28, 2018

• $100 if registered between Monday, July 29, 2019 through Sunday, August 4, 2019

• No registration accepted afterSunday, August 4, 2019

• Orientation is required for parents of campers
(Tim will contact parents regarding date)

Camp cost: This will cover the cost of food, supplies, and a camp T-shirt. Additional monetary or food donations are kindly appreciated. Unlike last year, there will be no optional packages. This is for two days and one overnight. Actual value of the camp is $150 per camper, but we are able to offer a reduced price thanks to our sponsors and volunteers.

Age Restriction: Youngest accepted is 10 years old. Oldest accepted are graduating 12th graders for Spring 2019. Please talk to us directly for possible age exceptions. They may be made on a case by case basis.

What to bring:

Change of clothes for 2 days

  • Shirts, sweaters, pants socks, underwear, etc.

  • Warm Jacket (can get very cold at night!)

Shoes appropriate for hiking

Toiletries (suggest using ziplock bag to protect against wildlife)

  • Deodorant, soap, toothbrush, toothpaste, sanitary supplies, sunscreen

Face cloth/Bath towel

  • There are no showers. There are only toilets and sinks.

Pillow

Medication (labeled)

Flashlight

Optional: games/cards, sunglasses, camera, bug spray, extra blanket, sleeping bag, tent, hat

Supplies provided by Camp TAY: tent, sleeping bag, water bottle, food, kitchenware, bowls and utensils
